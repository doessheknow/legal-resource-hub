Welcome to My ECVC Paralegal Research Repository

This repository is a collection of research, tools, and resources I have compiled in preparation for a role as an ECVC Paralegal. It covers key topics relevant to the emerging companies and venture capital space, including Delaware corporate law, venture capital financing, due diligence, securities compliance, equity incentive plan administration, and paralegal tools commonly used in this practice.

Research & Resources

  • Corporate Law & Governance: Overview of Delaware corporate law and best practices for managing corporate governance, including formation and qualification documents for Delaware corporations, and sample board resolutions and stockholder actions.
  • Due Diligence & Transaction Management: A comprehensive checklist and guidelines for performing due diligence in venture capital and M&A transactions, including essential documents and best practices for managing data rooms and transaction workflows.
  • Securities Compliance & Blue Sky Research: An introduction to Blue Sky laws and the paralegal’s role in securities compliance during venture capital financing, along with a guide to Blue Sky filings and state-level securities compliance.
  • Equity Incentive Plans: Insights into equity plan administration, tracking stock options, and ensuring compliance with federal securities laws. Includes sample documents and checklists for managing stock options and equity awards.
  • Tools & Technologies for Paralegals: Resources on essential software and tools for ECVC paralegals, such as Carta for equity plan administration, Google Docs and Box for collaboration, and DocuSign for electronic document execution.

Tools and Resources Showcase

  • Document Automation Tools: Tools to streamline the creation and management of legal documents, including resources for automating contract generation and document templates for corporate formations.
  • Legal Research Databases: Access to platforms like LexisNexis and Westlaw for comprehensive legal research on corporate law, securities, and IP matters.
  • Collaboration Tools: Tools like Google Docs and DocuSign for efficient document management and collaboration within legal teams, particularly useful for due diligence, M&A transactions, and compliance filings.
