ECVC Paralegal Research & Resources

Exploring Legal Research and Tools for Emerging Companies & Venture Capital

Welcome

This page is a collection of research, tools, and resources compiled to support an ECVC Paralegal role. It covers key topics relevant to emerging companies, venture capital financing, and corporate governance, including Delaware corporate law, equity incentive plans, due diligence processes, and securities compliance. Whether you're drafting corporate formation documents, managing due diligence, or navigating securities laws, you'll find valuable tools and research here. Please note, nothing on this page constitutes legal advice. Always consult a licensed legal professional for specific legal guidance.

Research & Resources

  • Corporate Law & Governance: Overview of Delaware corporate law, including resources on managing corporate records, formation documents, stockholder actions, and compliance with corporate governance standards.
  • Venture Capital Financing & Due Diligence: Key steps and checklists for performing due diligence in venture capital deals, including a focus on financials, corporate structure, intellectual property, and stockholder agreements.
  • Securities Compliance & Blue Sky Research: An introduction to Blue Sky laws and the paralegal's role in managing state and federal securities filings during venture capital financing.
  • Equity Incentive Plan Administration: Resources for understanding the administration of stock options and equity incentive plans, including compliance with federal securities laws.

Tools & Software Showcase

  • Document Automation Tools: Tools to streamline the creation and management of legal documents, including Docassemble for automated document generation, and templates for Delaware corporate formation.
  • Legal Research Databases: Access legal research platforms like LexisNexis and Westlaw to conduct comprehensive research on corporate law, securities, and due diligence.
  • Document Collaboration: Tools like Google Docs and DocuSign enable real-time collaboration on legal documents and secure e-signatures, which are vital in venture capital transactions.
  • Equity Management Software: Platforms like Carta help manage equity incentive plans and stock options, ensuring compliance with corporate governance standards and federal securities laws.
